About us

Who are we?

Premiumreal is a financial technology company enabling zero-fee cross-border money transfer across the world. Premiumreal saw a gap in the cross-border money transfer industry among many individuals, especially Africans and so it was established. Premiumreal is regulated by Fintrac of Canada. Presently, Premiumreal provides easier means for people to carry out money transfers from Canada to Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, UK and across Europe with zero-fee charge.This enables users, especially Africans in Canada and other parts of the world to send and receive money across borders without being charged.

Our Mission

Premiumreal aims at creating a suitable, easy and most importantly, a zero-fee cross-border money transfers for its users. Premiumreal understands that you would want to send money to your family members, friends, pay for a product, buy a property, pay for a seminar or training and the need for a seamless money transfer across borders without the fear of losing your money is the reason why Premiumreal technologies was established.
